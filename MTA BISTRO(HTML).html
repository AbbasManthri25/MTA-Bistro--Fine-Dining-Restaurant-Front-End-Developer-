Our Story

At Bella Vista, we believe that dining is more than just a meal—it's an experience that engages all your senses. Founded in 2015 by renowned chef Marco Antonelli, our restaurant has become a destination for food lovers seeking exceptional cuisine in an elegant atmosphere.

Our philosophy centers around using the finest locally-sourced ingredients, preparing each dish with meticulous attention to detail, and creating memorable moments for every guest who walks through our doors.

Chef Marco Antonelli

With over 20 years of culinary experience in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, Chef Marco brings his passion for authentic flavors and innovative techniques to every plate.
